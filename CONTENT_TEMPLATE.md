## Tips for Writing Content

### Headlines
- Focus on benefits, not features
- Use action words
- Keep it under 10 words
- Make it specific and compelling

### Descriptions
- Write in active voice
- Focus on how you solve customer problems
- Use simple language, avoid jargon
- Include a call to action

### Testimonials
- Use real customer quotes when possible
- Include specific details (what service, what result)
- Vary the length and tone
- Always get permission to use customer names/photos

### FAQ Answers
- Be concise but complete
- Anticipate follow-up questions
- Link to relevant pages for more info
- Keep tone friendly and helpful

### SEO Best Practices
- Include target keywords naturally
- Write for humans first, search engines second
- Keep meta descriptions under 150 characters
- Use unique descriptions for each page
